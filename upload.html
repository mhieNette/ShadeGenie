How to check your undertone and skin tone

  • Look at the veins on your wrist: greenish veins suggest warm undertones, bluish/purple veins suggest cool undertones, and both suggest neutral.
  • Notice how your skin reacts to the sun: if you tan easily, you may have a warm or olive undertone; if you burn, you may have a cool undertone.
  • Hold white paper next to your face in natural light: yellow/golden reflection means warm, pink/rosy means cool, and neither means neutral.
